Why must the permit be released in a finally block, and what production symptom appears if line 07 throws without it?

You

Otherwise failed handlers leak capacity, eventually preventing new requests from acquiring permits.

Correct. One detail to add:

Cancellation is also an exceptional exit. The invariant is one release for every successful acquire across all exits.
